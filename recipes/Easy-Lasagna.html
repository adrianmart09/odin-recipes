Easy Lasagna

Home

Ingredients required:

This recipe is the most delicious lasagna you will ever make! It contains many ingredients but all are necessary if you want the full satisfaction for your taste buds! Feel free to alter any flavours and spices if you dislike them, but don't change it too much.

  • 1 lb Ground Beef-Ground. Turkey for a lighter option
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of cooked noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a portion of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Top with a layer of mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

    13. And now you have a delicious, homemade lasagna to enjoy! Feel free to share this recipe with your family and friends
