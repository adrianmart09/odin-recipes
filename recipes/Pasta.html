Red Sauce Pasta

Home

Ingredients required:

This pasta recipe is simple, hearty and packed with flavour! It comes together quickly and is perfect for a weeknight dinner. Feel free to swap the sauce or add your favourite toppings, but keep the core ingredients for the best result.

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat a little olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour the marinara sauce into the skillet and stir in the oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it evenly with the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving (optional).

    6. And now you have a delicious, homemade pasta dish to enjoy! Feel free to share this recipe with your family and friends
